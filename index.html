What We Offer
Complete Water
Solutions

From the first geophysical scan to final water delivery — we manage the entire process end-to-end. One team, one call, complete solution for agricultural, residential and industrial clients.

Geophysical Survey

Advanced ground scanning pinpoints optimal borehole locations and subsurface water-bearing formations, minimising drilling risk and cost before we break ground.

Water Borehole Drilling

Professional water borehole drilling up to 250 metres in both soft and hard formations. 6.5" continuous diameter with overburden options of 9", 10" and 12".

Sedimentary Drilling

Specialist sedimentary formation drilling up to 250 metres using precision-calibrated equipment suited to the varied geological conditions across Southern Africa.

Pump Installation

Supply and full installation of hand pumps and submersible pump sets. Every installation is pressure-tested, commissioned and warranted before handover to the client.

Overhead Tank Construction

Construction of elevated water storage tanks built to SANS standards, engineered for long-term structural integrity in residential, commercial and agricultural settings.

Full Water Supply

Complete end-to-end delivery from borehole to tap. Tank construction, pipework, and commissioning — we don't leave until the water flows reliably at your site.
