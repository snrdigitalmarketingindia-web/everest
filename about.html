About Everest Drilling

South Africa's Borehole Drilling Specialists

Who We Are

Company Overview

Everest Drilling (Pty) Ltd is a South African borehole drilling and water solutions company with operational branches in the Eastern Cape, Durban (KwaZulu-Natal), and Johannesburg (Gauteng). The company provides complete turnkey water supply services — from geophysical survey through to borehole drilling, pump installation, and overhead tank construction — for residential, agricultural, commercial, and industrial clients across South Africa.

Our approach centres on understanding each client's water supply requirements and geological conditions before any rig is deployed. We manage the full project scope under a single point of contact, from initial assessment through to project handover.

Why Choose Everest

What Sets Us Apart

Survey-First Methodology

We conduct a geophysical electromagnetic and resistivity survey before any drilling begins. This maps subsurface water-bearing formations and helps identify the optimal drill point before any rig is deployed.

Complete Turnkey Service

One contractor manages the full project scope: geophysical survey, drilling, pump installation, and overhead tank construction. No subcontracting, single point of contact throughout.

Industrial-Grade Equipment

Heavy-duty truck-mounted drilling rigs capable of penetrating soft and hard formations. Equipment details available on the Technology page.

Nationwide Coverage

Operational branches in the Eastern Cape (HQ), Durban, and Johannesburg, with the ability to mobilise to agricultural and rural sites across South Africa's nine provinces.

Agricultural & Rural Experience

Extensive experience on farm operations, game farms, smallholdings, and rural community projects. We understand the operational requirements of agricultural water supply.
