Service Areas Across South Africa

Everest Drilling maintains operational branches across South Africa, serving residential, agricultural, commercial, and industrial clients. The Eastern Cape serves as the company's primary operational base, with active branches in Durban and Johannesburg. The company also serves the Western Cape and can mobilise to projects across South Africa's nine provinces.

Why Choose Everest Drilling
Across South Africa?

Whether your project is in Johannesburg, Durban, East London, Pretoria, or a remote rural site, Everest Drilling brings the same standard of equipment, expertise, and service. Our turnkey approach — geophysical survey, drilling, pump installation, and tank construction — means a single point of contact from survey through to commissioning.

Industrial Rigs

Heavy-duty rotary percussion rigs capable of drilling through hard granite, quartzite, sandstone, and dolerite formations found across South Africa.
